This week we have look at our new partner, [Wonder Studios](https://wonderstudios.com/), a next-generation entertainment company built for the AI era,

**Wonder Studios** is an AI-native film and content studio working across original series, short films, features, music videos, documentaries, commercials, and branded content. The company brings together creative production, original storytelling, creator technology, and community-led talent discovery.

#### AI and the Changing Creative Landscape

AI is moving quickly through film, television, advertising, music videos, documentaries, and branded content. Across the creative industries, production teams are experimenting with new tools for image generation, editing, voice, animation, visual effects, world-building, and pre-visualisation.

The creative landscape is changing as a result. Smaller teams can now test ideas faster. Independent filmmakers can produce more ambitious visual work. Brands can develop content with shorter timelines. Studios and creators are also rethinking how stories are funded, made, distributed, and discovered.

This shift is already visible across the wider entertainment and media sector. AI-assisted production is becoming part of conversations around the future of film, advertising, creator platforms, intellectual property, and audience development. It is also raising important questions about creative skills, authorship, ethics, and how human direction works alongside new technology.

## Introducing Wonder Studios




The business was founded in 2025 by award-winning filmmaker [Justin Hackney](https://wonderstudios.com/about) and marketplace entrepreneur [Xavier Collins](https://wonderstudios.com/about). Their work brings together two important strands of the current creative economy: deep filmmaking experience and the platform thinking needed to support a new generation of creators.

Wonder Studios operates across several connected areas. Its [Creative Studio](https://wonderstudios.com/) produces work for brands, studios, and artists, combining live action, animation, AI workflows, and hybrid production. Its original film work develops and produces new stories for a changing entertainment market. Its [Wonder App](https://wonderstudios.com/) supports filmmakers, artists, storytellers, and creative technologists by helping them connect, collaborate, find work, and get discovered.

Through [Wonder TV](https://tv.wonderstudios.com/), the company also curates films from its creator community. The platform gives visibility to emerging talent through editorial selection rather than relying only on algorithms or follower counts. Its projects include [Beyond the Loop](https://tv.wonderstudios.com/), an anthology of short films created with award-winning filmmakers and storytellers.
