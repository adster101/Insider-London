In this episode of *The Knowledge Exchange*, we sit down with Rory Brimmer, Managing Director at Turo UK, to unpack how graduates can truly stand out in today’s hyper‑competitive tech landscape. Rory shares his journey from university to leading one of the world’s biggest peer‑to‑peer car‑sharing platforms, offering a candid look at what early‑career talent often gets wrong — and what the best candidates do differently.

We explore why communication is the most underrated skill in tech, how graduates can demonstrate attitude and adaptability, and why turning down a “safe” corporate path opened the door to a far more dynamic career. Rory also reflects on the real value of studying abroad, the rise of AI-driven decision-making, and what universities should teach next to prepare students for fast-moving, data-driven environments.
